WAMO Bot

AI Chatbot for Role-Based Training & Process Adoption · American Heart Association

screenshot coming soon — drop images/wamo.png to populate

During the rollout of Workfront and Bynder to 5,000+ users at the American Heart Association, I identified a discoverability gap: two years of role-specific training content existed across nine user personas — housed in SharePoint and surfaced through Workfront and Bynder — but staff couldn't find the right resource for their role on demand, driving support volume and slowing adoption. I originated the concept for WAMO Bot as an access layer on top of that existing content infrastructure and led end-to-end product ownership from discovery through pilot authorization.
