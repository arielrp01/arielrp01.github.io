Agentic Verified RAG
for Health Insurance Plan QA

A document-grounded question-answering system for health insurance plan documents, built iteratively to solve specific, diagnosable failure modes. The core is a deterministic solver with 5-way intent routing, anchor-based re-retrieval to eliminate plan-table row drift, a numeric verifier that checks dollar amounts and percentages token-by-token before returning, and a safety gate that blocks medical triage queries while allowing coverage questions using identical clinical terminology. An agentic verify → repair → retry loop wraps the solver for demos, adding traceability without affecting scored results. Improved from 93.9% (31/33) to 100% (33/33) on a hand-crafted gold-key evaluation set through targeted architectural iteration.
